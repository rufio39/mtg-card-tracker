Magic is a turn-based strategy game.

The players start out with 20 health points and will have a deck already constructed consisting of at least 60 cards.

Building a deck

a deck consit of of a number of cards. Each card has a name, a cost, and a description. Each deck contains a minimum of 60 cards ranging from creatures to spells.

Creatures

types

there are many types of creatures. Each creature has a name, a cost, a description, and a type. The types are: angel, demon, dragon, elf, giant, snake, and vampire are just some of the types.

Creatures are the most important cards in Magic. They have a name, a cost, a description, and a power and toughness. Creatures can attack{10} and be attacked.
